OUR STORY

Founded on the principles of automotive excellence and uncompromising luxury, APEX LUXE MOTORS has been the premier destination for discerning enthusiasts seeking the world's finest vehicles. Our passion for performance and dedication to service excellence defines every aspect of our business.

2010

The Beginning

APEX LUXE MOTORS was founded with a vision to redefine luxury automotive retail. Starting with a curated selection of European supercars, we established our reputation for excellence.

2015

Expansion

Opened our flagship showroom and service center, featuring state-of-the-art facilities and expanding our inventory to include the world's most exclusive automotive brands.

2018

Innovation

Launched our concierge services and became early adopters of luxury electric vehicles, positioning ourselves at the forefront of automotive innovation.

2021

EV Revolution

Opened our dedicated EV Lab, showcasing the latest in electric performance technology and sustainable luxury transportation solutions.

2024

Future Forward

Continuing to lead the luxury automotive industry with cutting-edge technology, unparalleled service, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
